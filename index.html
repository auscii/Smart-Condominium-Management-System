Emergency Procedures

Fire Emergency Evacuation

  1. Stay calm and alert others in your area
  2. Feel doors before opening - if hot, use alternate exit
  3. Stay low to avoid smoke inhalation
  4. Use stairs, never elevators
  5. Proceed to designated assembly area
  6. Call building security and report your location
  7. Do not re-enter the building until cleared

Earthquake Safety

  1. Drop to your hands and knees
  2. Cover your head and neck
  3. Take shelter under sturdy furniture
  4. Stay away from windows and heavy objects
  5. After shaking stops, evacuate carefully
  6. Gather at designated assembly area
  7. Check for injuries and report damages

Medical Emergency Response

  1. Assess the situation for safety
  2. Call 911 for life-threatening conditions
  3. Contact building security
  4. Do not move injured person unless necessary
  5. Apply first aid if trained
  6. Wait for emergency responders
  7. Provide emergency access to responders

Lockdown Protocol

  1. Proceed to nearest secure room immediately
  2. Lock and barricade doors
  3. Turn off lights and stay away from windows
  4. Silence your phone
  5. Remain quiet and hidden
  6. Wait for official "All Clear" announcement
  7. Do not open doors for anyone except security
